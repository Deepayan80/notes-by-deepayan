Why Most People Never Compound Wealth

Jan 28, 2026 · 6 min read

Compounding is simple to understand, yet painfully hard to practice. Most people fail not because they don’t know the math, but because they underestimate time and overestimate discipline.

The real enemy of compounding is impatience. We want results now, not ten years later. This bias quietly destroys long-term outcomes.

Wealth, like character, grows slowly. Almost invisibly.

— Deepayan
